Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas

Description

Sheet pan chicken fajitas with juicy strips of chicken, bell peppers and onions tossed in a delicious homemade fajita seasoning.

Great as a single meal or preparing meals for the whole family for the week.

Ingredients

  • chicken breast strips
  • bell peppers, onions and zucchini strips if you like
  • fajita seasoning
  • olive oil
  • lime juice
  • tortillas
  • Toppings:
    • cilantro
    • avocado slices
    • salsa
    • sour cream
    • cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 425°F.
  2. Toss chicken, veggies, olive oil and lime juice in bowl.
  3. Add fajita seasoning and stir.
  4. Spread evenly on sheet pan
  5. Cook for 10 mins
  6. Remove from oven and flip
  7. Cook another 5-10 minutes or until chicken is fully cooked (165°F internal).
  8. Serve with tortillas and any toppings.
