Fruit and Yogurt Parfaits

Description

A great way to start the day with a healthy grab and go breakfast. Make these fruit and yogurt parfaits ahead and have them ready for when you head out the door.

With just a few simple ingredients you will leave your belly happy, taste buds satisfied and your body ready to take on the day.

Ingredients

  • your favorite yogurt, I prefer vanilla greek yogurt
  • crunchy granola and/or nuts
  • Fruits:
    • berries (strawberry and blueberry)
    • peaches
    • pineapples
    • get creative and try something new

Steps

  1. Add 3 tablespoons yogurt to mason jar.
  2. Layer fruit on top.
  3. Add 3 more tablespoons of yogurt.
  4. Layer fruit once more.
  5. Add your favorite granola and/or nuts on top.
  6. Refrigerate and eat when ready.
